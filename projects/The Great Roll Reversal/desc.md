# The Great Roll Reversal

!["Cover Art"](<projects/The Great Roll Reversal/bread_cover_art.png>)

## DESCRIPTION

In this space exploration & platforming fighting game, play as a bread roll fixing the impurity of the universe.. by turning everything into bread rolls. This was our first game jam and we hope you enjoy! (ps, after the jam end we'll be making a post jam version with your feedback)

## KNOWN BUGS

- Dying in "level" phases prevents completion of said level

- Intro cutscene not visible at certain screen scales

- Outro cutscene not visible

- Press e to land appears at times when it is not meant to (close to sun/ level phases)

## FLYING CONTROLS

W: Thrust

A-D: Rotate

M: Map

C: Toggle Camera

## FIGHTING CONTROLS

A-D: Move

SPACE: Jump

LEFT CLICK: Shoot

TIPS (Avoid this is you want to go in blind):

- When taking off prioritize going horizontal rather than vertical

- You probably want to go through the planets in anticlockwise order, as this is the order of easiest to hardest.
